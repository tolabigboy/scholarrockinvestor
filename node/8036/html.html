ITEM 4.OWNERSHIP

 

The percentages of beneficial ownership stated herein and on each Reporting Person’s cover page to this Schedule 13G are based on a total of 33,638,829 Shares outstanding as of November 4, 2020, as reported by the Issuer in its Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the quarterly period ended September 30, 2020, filed by the Issuer with the Securities and Exchange Commission on November 9, 2020. The cover page to this Schedule 13G for each Reporting Person is incorporated by reference in its entirety into this Item 4. As of the date hereof, the Reporting Persons, in the aggregate, beneficially own 312,951 Shares, representing approximately 0.93% of such class of securities.

 

Dr. Springer is the beneficial owner of a total of 312,951 Shares, representing approximately 0.93% of the outstanding Shares and consisting of 312,951 Shares held by TAS. Dr. Springer disclaims beneficial ownership of the Shares held by TAS, except to the extent of his pecuniary interest therein.

 

TAS is the beneficial owner of a total of 312,951 Shares, representing approximately 0.93% of the outstanding Shares. TAS holds all such Shares directly. Dr. Springer is the sole managing member of TAS.
