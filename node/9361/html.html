Item 7.01Regulation FD Disclosure.

 

Scholar Rock Holding Corporation is aware of media reports indicating the closure of Silicon Valley Bank (“SVB”) and appointment of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation as receiver. Scholar Rock Holding Corporation and its subsidiaries (collectively, the “Company”) currently maintain cash and cash equivalents, in the single digit millions of U.S. Dollars with SVB. The Company had cash, cash equivalents and marketable securities of approximately $315 million as of December 31, 2022, and the remainder of the Company’s cash, cash equivalents and marketable securities are held at other banking institutions, which are not directly exposed to liquidity concerns at SVB.
