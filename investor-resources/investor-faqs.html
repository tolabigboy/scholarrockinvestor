When was Scholar Rock founded and where is Scholar Rock incorporated?
Scholar Rock was founded in October 2012 and is currently incorporated in the state of Delaware.
From where did “Scholar Rock” derive its name?
Scholar rocks have unusual patterns of erosion and wear, which are believed to reveal inner strength and truth. This capacity of scholar rocks to elicit deep insight, and their visual similarity to protein structures, reflect the Company’s quest to discover breakthrough medicines and is why the company is called Scholar Rock.
Where is Scholar Rock’s corporate headquarters located?
Scholar Rock is headquartered at:
620 Memorial Drive, 2nd Floor
Cambridge, MA 02139
When is Scholar Rock’s fiscal year-end?
Scholar Rock’s fiscal year ends on December 31st.
When did Scholar Rock go public?
Scholar Rock began trading on the NASDAQ Global Select Market on May 24, 2018.
On which exchange is Scholar Rock listed and what is the ticker symbol?
Shares of Scholar Rock are traded on NASDAQ Global Select Market under the symbol “SRRK”.
Does Scholar Rock pay dividend or offer a dividend reinvestment program?
Scholar Rock does not pay a dividend or offer a dividend reinvestment program.
